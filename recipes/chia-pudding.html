Chia Pudding

Description

3-Ingredient Chia Pudding is made with almond milk, chia seeds & sweetener of choice

Ingredients

  • Chia seeds
  • Milk
  • Honey

Steps

  1. Add your milk of choice to a jar and sprinkle in the chia seed.
  2. After stirring the milk and seeds together, drizzle in honey or sweetener of choice.
  3. Cover and refrigerate the pudding for at least 2 hours or overnight.
